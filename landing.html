Main Characters

Jin Sakai

The Ghost - Protagonist

A samurai warrior and the last survivor of his clan after the Mongol invasion. Jin must abandon traditional samurai honor to become "The Ghost" and use unconventional tactics to liberate Tsushima.

  • Role: Playable protagonist
  • Clan: Sakai Clan
  • Weapon: Katana (Sakai Storm)
  • Fighting Style: Samurai & Ghost techniques

Lord Shimura

Jin's Uncle - Jito of Tsushima

The lord of Tsushima and Jin's uncle who raised him after his father's death. A strict adherent to the samurai code of honor who becomes conflicted with Jin's Ghost tactics.

  • Role: Mentor & Antagonist
  • Position: Jito (Military Governor)
  • Philosophy: Traditional samurai honor
  • Relationship: Guardian uncle to Jin

Yuna

Thief & Ally

A resourceful thief who helps Jin after the initial Mongol invasion. She teaches Jin the ways of deception and becomes instrumental in building the Ghost legend.

  • Role: Key ally & guide
  • Skills: Stealth, survival, information gathering
  • Motivation: Protecting her brother Taka
  • Contribution: Teaches Jin Ghost tactics

Masako Adachi

The Warrior Woman

A skilled archer and noblewoman seeking revenge for the murder of her family. One of Jin's most formidable allies in the fight against the Mongols.

  • Role: Ally & quest giver
  • Specialty: Archery master
  • Motivation: Revenge for family
  • Status: Clan Adachi survivor

Enemy Types

Khotun Khan

Main Antagonist - Mongol Leader

The cunning and ruthless leader of the Mongol invasion of Tsushima. A brilliant strategist who uses psychological warfare and studies his enemies to exploit their weaknesses.

  • Type: Boss / Main Villain
  • Tactics: Psychological warfare, strategy
  • Weakness: Overconfidence in his methods
  • Threat Level: Extreme

Mongol Swordsmen

Basic Infantry

Standard Mongol warriors armed with swords. They attack in groups and are the most common enemy type throughout Tsushima.

  • Weapons: Sword, occasionally shield
  • Tactics: Aggressive frontal attacks
  • Counter: Stone Stance recommended
  • Threat Level: Low-Medium

Mongol Brutes

Heavy Infantry

Large, heavily armored warriors wielding massive weapons. They have high health and can break through defenses with powerful attacks.

  • Weapons: Heavy blades, hammers
  • Tactics: Heavy unblockable attacks
  • Counter: Moon Stance, dodging
  • Threat Level: High

Mongol Archers

Ranged Support

Ranged enemies who attack from a distance. They prioritize staying away from melee combat and can be deadly if ignored.

  • Weapons: Bow and arrows
  • Tactics: Distance attacks, repositioning
  • Counter: Ranged attacks or quick approach
  • Threat Level: Medium

Mongol Spearmen

Defensive Infantry

Warriors wielding long spears with excellent reach. They maintain distance and use their range advantage to keep enemies at bay.

  • Weapons: Spear or polearm
  • Tactics: Range attacks, defensive stance
  • Counter: Water Stance breaks defense
  • Threat Level: Medium

Ronin

Elite Samurai Enemies

Masterless samurai who have betrayed their lords to serve the Mongols. They are highly skilled swordsmen who use advanced techniques.

  • Weapons: Katana
  • Tactics: Advanced swordplay, counters
  • Counter: Perfect parries, Wind Stance
  • Threat Level: Very High

Gameplay Elements

Combat Stances

Master four distinct stances: Stone (swordsmen), Water (shields), Wind (spears), and Moon (brutes). Switch dynamically to counter different enemy types.

Ghost Weapons

Utilize kunai, smoke bombs, sticky bombs, and wind chimes to fight dishonorably but effectively against overwhelming odds.

Standoffs

Engage in dramatic one-on-one duels where timing is everything. A perfectly timed strike can eliminate multiple enemies instantly.

Archery

Master the half-bow for ranged combat. Unlock special arrows including explosive and poison varieties for tactical advantages.

Ghost Techniques

Learn assassination techniques, chain kills, and terror tactics that strike fear into your enemies and turn the tide of battle.

Guiding Wind

Follow the wind to discover hidden locations, hot springs, bamboo strikes, and Inari shrines across the island of Tsushima.
